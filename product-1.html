Description

Succumb to the fiery delight of our Spicy Pork on Rice, a dish crafted for those who treasure a bit of zest in their meal. Perfectly seasoned pork slices are marinated in a spicy blend of chili peppers, garlic, and soy sauce, then stir-fried until golden and tantalizing. Served over a bed of steamed jasmine rice, each bite offers a crisp yet tender texture, bursting with flavors that are both robust and deeply satisfying.

  • Chili Paste
  • Garlic
  • Green Onions
  • Fresh Red Chili

  • Chop the pork
  • Prepare the rice
  • Cut in vegetables
  • Cook the pork
